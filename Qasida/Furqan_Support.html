Frequently asked questions

How do I connect my Azan speaker?

Open Furqan Quran, enable Bluetooth on your phone, then follow the in-app pairing steps. Keep the speaker nearby and powered on until pairing completes.

The speaker does not appear in the list. What should I try?

Restart Bluetooth on your phone, power-cycle the speaker, and ensure it is not connected to another device. Then scan again from the app.

How do I set or adjust Azan times?

Use the Azan time settings in the app to configure schedules and parameters. Save your changes and confirm they sync to the speaker when prompted.

Can I customize lighting and display behavior?

Yes. Furqan Quran provides options to adjust lighting and display modes for supported hardware. Available options depend on your speaker model.

Playback or BLE connection drops sometimes. Any tips?

Keep the app updated, avoid long distances or heavy wireless interference, and disable battery optimizations that may restrict Bluetooth in the background.

常见问题

如何连接宣礼音箱？

打开 Furqan Quran，在手机系统设置中开启蓝牙，然后按应用内配对引导操作。请保持音箱开机并靠近手机直至配对完成。

列表里找不到音箱怎么办？

可尝试关闭并重新打开手机蓝牙、重启音箱，并确认音箱未与其他设备保持连接，然后在应用中重新扫描。

如何设置或调整宣礼时间？

在应用的宣礼时间相关设置中配置时间与参数，保存后按提示确认已同步到音箱。

可以自定义灯光和显示吗？

可以。Furqan Quran 为支持的硬件提供灯光与显示模式等选项，具体以您的音箱型号为准。

播放或 BLE 连接偶尔中断怎么办？

请保持应用为最新版本，尽量靠近设备并减少强无线干扰；如系统有省电策略，请避免限制本应用在后台使用蓝牙。
